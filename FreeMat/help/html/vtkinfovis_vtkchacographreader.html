vtkChacoGraphReader

Section: Visualization Toolkit Infovis Classes

Usage

vtkChacoGraphReader reads in files in the Chaco format into a vtkGraph. An example is the following <code> 10 13 2 6 10 1 3 2 4 8 3 5 4 6 10 1 5 7 6 8 3 7 9 8 10 1 5 9 </code> The first line specifies the number of vertices and edges in the graph. Each additional line contains the vertices adjacent to a particular vertex. In this example, vertex 1 is adjacent to 2, 6 and 10, vertex 2 is adjacent to 1 and 3, etc. Since Chaco ids start at 1 and VTK ids start at 0, the vertex ids in the vtkGraph will be 1 less than the Chaco ids.

To create an instance of class vtkChacoGraphReader, simply invoke its constructor as follows 

  obj = vtkChacoGraphReader
