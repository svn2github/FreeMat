vtkOpenGLLightingPainter

Section: Visualization Toolkit Rendering Classes

Usage

This painter manages lighting. Ligting is disabled when rendering points/lines and no normals are present or rendering Polygons/TStrips and representation is points and no normals are present.

To create an instance of class vtkOpenGLLightingPainter, simply invoke its constructor as follows
