vtkTensorProbeWidget

Section: Visualization Toolkit Widget Classes

Usage

The class is used to probe tensors on a trajectory. The representation (vtkTensorProbeRepresentation) is free to choose its own method of rendering the tensors. For instance vtkEllipsoidTensorProbeRepresentation renders the tensors as ellipsoids. The interactions of the widget are controlled by the left mouse button. A left click on the tensor selects it. It can dragged around the trajectory to probe the tensors on it. For instance dragging the ellipsoid around with vtkEllipsoidTensorProbeRepresentation will manifest itself with the ellipsoid shape changing as needed along the trajectory.

To create an instance of class vtkTensorProbeWidget, simply invoke its constructor as follows 

  obj = vtkTensorProbeWidget
